I am **Karthik Babu Nambiar**, a PhD Scholar in **MOON LAB**, IISER Bhopal.  
My work spans **Computer Vision**, **Deep Learning**, **Autonomous Driving**, and **Robotics**.  
I specialize in sim-to-real transfer, real-time perception, and autonomous navigation on ground and aerial platforms.

I completed my **BS–MS in Physics** from IISER Bhopal (2022), followed by an **MSc in Data Science & Management** from **IIT Indore** and **IIM Indore** (2024).  
I previously worked on multiple autonomous driving projects, robot learning modules, and intelligent sampling for field robotics.

## Experience

### **Project Associate — Moon Lab, IISER Bhopal** (Oct 2023 — Dec 2023)
- Object detection & decision-making on **Unitree Go1** quadruped using RGB + thermal cameras.

### **Project Associate — TIH-iHub Drishti, IIT Jodhpur** (Jan 2023 — Sep 2023)
- Implemented autonomous driving models on a 4-wheel differential drive rover.  
- Conducted real-world deployment and testing.

### **Project Associate — Moon Lab, IISER Bhopal** (Oct 2022 — Dec 2022)
- Developed CNN-based autonomous driving model using stereo cameras for waypoint prediction.

### **Senior Executive – Data Science Consultant — Deloitte** (Jun 2022 — Sep 2022)
- Application development for market risk and stress testing.
